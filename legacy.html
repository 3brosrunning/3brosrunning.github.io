Honoring Our Legacy Runners

We are proud to honor the remarkable runners who have completed the Toughest 10K Kemah year after year. Your dedication to conquering this challenging course has made you an enduring part of our community and history. Each year, you show resilience, strength, and unwavering commitment, inspiring us all.

Below is a list of our esteemed legacy runners—those who have embraced the Toughest 10K Kemah challenge time and time again, building a legacy that elevates this race. Thank you for being an essential part of our journey. We look forward to cheering you on in many more races to come!

With heartfelt gratitude,
The 3 Bros Running Company Team
