Our History is Written in Your Miles

Since 2004, the Seabrook Lucky Trails has been more than just a race—it's a gathering of a community that spans decades. Whether you've joined us once or you've been with us since the very beginning, your footsteps are part of the Seabrook story.

To our legacy runners: you are the heart of this event. We take great pride in tracking every mile you've run through the trails of Rex Meador Park. This board is our way of saying thank you for your incredible loyalty and passion.
