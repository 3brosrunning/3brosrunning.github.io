La Porte Legacy Runners

Celebrating Endurance on the Fred Hartman Bridge

The La Porte By the Bay Half Marathon is the grand finale of the Texas Bridge Series—a 13.1 mile test of endurance over the iconic Fred Hartman Bridge.

Legacy runners return year after year, taking on the sweeping incline and unpredictable winds that make this course one of Texas’ most unforgettable challenges.

We honor those who have completed this race five, ten, or even more times—especially our top legacy champions. Your persistence and strength inspire the running community.
