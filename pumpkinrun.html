To Our Founding Runners

When we held the first Pumpkin Run in Orange back in 2022, we hoped it would become something special. Because of your support, it did. Whether you've joined us for one year or have been with us for every single race, you are the heartbeat of this event.

As we move our tradition to Port Arthur, we want to look back and say thank you. Your consistency and dedication to the 3 Bros Running Company family means the world to us.

Special recognition to our "Original Legacy" athletes who have completed every race since 2022!
